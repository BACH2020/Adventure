The Roller Coaster of Probability

Six Flags Magic Mountain is an amusement park known as the thrill capital of the world! It has over 30 extreme roller coasters and many fun games to play! You have the opportunity to win FREE tickets AND fast passes IF you choose the right door to enter. Let's get started!

If you guess the name of the rollercoaster then you will win 2 FREE admission tickets and 2 FREE fast passes. You are only allowed one attempt and you will have the opportunity to open the other doors if you answer correctly. BONUS! The descriptions of the rollercoasters contain lyrics from popular songs. If you guess the song or name of the singer/band you will receive the monetary value of the tickets and fast passes.
